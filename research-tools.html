Library Programs and Learning

School Library Learning Opportunities

  • Header
    • Welcome to our library where students read, explore, create, and discover new ideas every day.
  • Main Content

    1. Reading Engagement

      Students are introduced to new genres through book trailers, book tastings, themed displays, and reading challenges that help build a lifelong love of reading.

    2. Research and Collaboration

      Students learn how to locate reliable information, use research tools, and collaborate with classmates to answer questions and complete projects.

    3. Science and Social Studies Exploration

      Through hands-on lessons and learning stations, students investigate important science and social studies concepts.

    4. STEM and Engineering Challenges

      Students participate in STEM activities and engineering stations that encourage creativity, problem-solving, teamwork, and innovation.
